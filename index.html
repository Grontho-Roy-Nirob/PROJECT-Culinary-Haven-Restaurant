2008 - Foundation

Established "Royal Taste" in Banani, Dhaka, with a vision to offer authentic Bangladeshi cuisine.

2012 - Expansion

Opened a second branch in Gulshan, Dhaka, to cater to a growing customer base.

Completely renovated the Banani branch to provide a more modern and comfortable dining experience.

2018 - Awards
